My project was to create pac-man. The player can move and collect food that increases the score, displayed in the lower left corner. 
The player has 3 lives and loses a life when it collides with one of the ghosts. If all the food is collected, the player wins. If the
player loses their 3 lives before collecting all the food, the game ends and there is the option to restart. 
Bugs:
- The ghosts don't move correctly
- When the game restarts, the food already eaten is not returned to the board
